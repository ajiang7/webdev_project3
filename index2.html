Exploring Vtubers

What are Vtubers?

Vtubers, or Virtual YouTubers, are online entertainers who use digital avatars to engage with their audiences. These avatars are typically animated using motion capture technology, allowing performers to express themselves in real-time while maintaining anonymity.

The concept of digital avatars representing content creators dates back to early internet forums and virtual reality experiments, but Vtubers as we know them today originated in Japan in the mid-2010s. Kizuna AI, often credited as the first major Vtuber, debuted in 2016 and set the stage for what would become a massive industry.

Over time, Vtubing has evolved beyond just streaming, with many performers producing high-quality music, animation, and scripted content. The fandom surrounding Vtubers has grown exponentially, with millions of viewers tuning in from around the world.

Who are Hololive and Cover Corp?

Hololive is one of the most prominent Vtuber agencies, managed by Cover Corporation. Established in 2016, Cover Corp initially focused on AR and VR technologies before shifting its focus to Vtubers.

The early success of Hololive was driven by its first-generation talents, who captivated audiences with their unique personalities and content. As demand for Vtuber content grew, Hololive expanded its roster, introducing new generations of talents and branching out into international markets.

Beyond YouTube streaming, Hololive has ventured into various multimedia projects, including anime collaborations, original music production, and large-scale live concerts.

Where is Hololive/Cover Corp Now?

Today, Hololive has expanded into a global phenomenon with multiple talent branches, including Hololive Japan, Hololive English, and Hololive Indonesia.

Hololive talents have amassed millions of subscribers across various platforms, making them some of the most influential online personalities in the digital entertainment space.

Cover Corporation continues to invest in new technologies to enhance the Vtubing experience, paving the way for the next evolution of digital entertainment.
